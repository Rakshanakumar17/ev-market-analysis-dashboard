⚡ Electric Vehicle Market Analysis Dashboard (2015–2023)

An interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing Electric Vehicle (EV) registration
trends using the Washington State Department of Licensing (DOL) EV Population
dataset.

🔍 Overview

This project explores how EV adoption has grown over time, which brands and
vehicle types dominate the market, and how EV driving range has evolved from
1996 to 2022. The goal was to turn raw vehicle registration data into a clean,
storytelling dashboard.

📊 Key Insights


Explosive recent growth — EV registrations stayed near-flat for decades,
then grew sharply after 2015, peaking at over 25K registrations around 2023.
Tesla dominates the market — Tesla leads with ~62K registered vehicles,
more than 4x the next closest brand (Nissan).
Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) lead adoption — BEVs make up 76.9% of
the market vs. 23.1% for Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs).
Range has improved dramatically — Average electric range rose from
~39 miles (1997) to a peak of 241 miles (2020), reflecting major battery
technology improvements over the past two decades.
Adoption is concentrated regionally — King County, WA alone accounts for
the largest share of registered EVs in the dataset (15,258+), highlighting
strong urban adoption patterns.


🛠️ Tools & Skills Used


Tableau — dashboard design, containers, interactive filtering, layout
Data cleaning & transformation — handling nulls, calculated fields
Data storytelling — structuring multiple views into one cohesive dashboard
